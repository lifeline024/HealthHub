Medicine and Disease List

Caution: Before using any medicine, please consult a doctor.

Paracetamol
Used to reduce fever and relieve pain.
Amoxicillin
An antibiotic used to treat a variety of bacterial infections.
Ibuprofen
Used to reduce fever, pain, and inflammation.
Aspirin
Used to relieve pain, reduce fever, and prevent blood clots.
Metformin
Used to treat type 2 diabetes.
Atorvastatin
Used to lower cholesterol and reduce the risk of heart disease.
Omeprazole
Used to treat acid reflux and ulcers.
Losartan
Used to treat high blood pressure and kidney disease.
Levothyroxine
Used to treat hypothyroidism.
Prednisone
A corticosteroid used to treat inflammation and autoimmune conditions.
Warfarin
Used as a blood thinner to prevent blood clots.
Gabapentin
Used to treat nerve pain and seizures.
Metoprolol
Used to treat high blood pressure and heart conditions.
Amlodipine
Used to treat high blood pressure and chest pain (angina).
Hydrochlorothiazide
A diuretic used to treat high blood pressure and fluid retention.
Ciprofloxacin
An antibiotic used to treat bacterial infections.
Clopidogrel
Used to prevent heart attacks and strokes.
Doxycycline
An antibiotic used to treat bacterial infections.
Tramadol
A pain reliever used for moderate to severe pain.
Fluoxetine
An antidepressant used to treat depression and anxiety disorders.
Insulin
Used to manage diabetes by controlling blood sugar levels.
Montelukast
Used to treat allergies and asthma.
Ranitidine
Used to reduce stomach acid and treat ulcers.
Simvastatin
Used to lower cholesterol levels.
Diazepam
Used to treat anxiety, muscle spasms, and seizures.
Furosemide
A diuretic used to treat fluid retention and high blood pressure.
Lisinopril
Used to treat high blood pressure and heart failure.
Hydrocortisone
Used to treat inflammation, allergies, and skin conditions.
Alprazolam
Used to treat anxiety and panic disorders.
Malaria
Caused by a mosquito bite, treated with Chloroquine and Artemisinin.
Diabetes
Caused by insulin resistance or insufficient insulin production, treated with Metformin.
Hypertension
Caused by high blood pressure, treated with Amlodipine and Losartan.
Asthma
Chronic inflammation of airways, treated with Montelukast and Salbutamol.
Pneumonia
Lung infection, treated with Amoxicillin and Azithromycin.
Tuberculosis
Bacterial infection, treated with Rifampin and Isoniazid.
COVID-19
Viral infection, managed with Remdesivir and Dexamethasone.
Common Cold
Viral infection, managed with Paracetamol and Antihistamines.
Influenza (Flu)
Viral infection, treated with Oseltamivir and supportive care.
HIV/AIDS
Viral infection, treated with Antiretroviral Therapy (ART).
Hepatitis B
Liver infection, treated with Tenofovir and Entecavir.
Hepatitis C
Liver infection, treated with Sofosbuvir and Ledipasvir.
Arthritis
Joint inflammation, treated with Ibuprofen and Methotrexate.
Gastritis
Stomach inflammation, treated with Omeprazole and Ranitidine.
Gout
Joint pain due to uric acid, treated with Allopurinol and Colchicine.
Epilepsy
Seizure disorder, treated with Gabapentin and Carbamazepine.
Alzheimer's Disease
Neurodegenerative disorder, managed with Donepezil and Memantine.
Depression
Mental health disorder, treated with Fluoxetine and Sertraline.
Anemia
Iron deficiency, treated with Iron supplements and Folic acid.
Kidney Stones
Mineral buildup, treated with Pain relievers and Hydration therapy.
UTI (Urinary Tract Infection)
Bacterial infection, treated with Ciprofloxacin and Nitrofurantoin.
